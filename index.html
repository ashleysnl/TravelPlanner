About TravelPlanner

Plan your trip, track your budget, and manage your itinerary — private, simple, and offline. No account required.

TravelPlanner is a local-first trip planner that stores your data in your browser on your device. There is no login, no cloud account, and no tracking built into the app.

Built by a Dad who wanted a simple, private trip planner.

  • Works on desktop and mobile
  • Offline-friendly and printable
  • Budget + itinerary in one place
